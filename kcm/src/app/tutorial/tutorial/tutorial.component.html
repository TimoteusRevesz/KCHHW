Shoe Pairing Game

How to Play:

  1. You'll be presented with a grid of face-down cards.
  2. Click on a card to reveal the hidden image of a shoe.
  3. Keep clicking on cards until you find a matching pair.
  4. When you find a pair, they will be removed from play.
  5. If the cards don't match, they will flip back over.
  6. Keep playing until you've matched all the shoes!
